JULY 29, 2014

Secure Credit Card System with Digital Form

LAShowroom.com is pleased to announce our new Secure Credit Card system with Digital Form. This system provides secure and convenient payment method, eliminating the need for faxes and CC authorization forms and promoting ease of use. We have taken great measures to ensure that all cardholder data is saved and viewed securely on our servers.

Below are 2 key features that this new system provides:

1. Secure Digital Form
Our secure digital form allows buyers to send credit card information via our check out page at the time of placing an order. Once the order is placed, the vendor will process the payment manually. Look for the "Secure Digital Form" option in the checkout page.

As a buyer, this new feature creates a more simple and speedy process to send payment information. It is a paperless alternative to the printable CC authorization form, completely removing the need for scans, emails and faxes. However, should you still prefer the traditional CC authorization form, the option is still available.

2. Saving Credit Cards for Checkout
Once the credit card information is entered, buyers will have the option to save this information for future use. Buyers may select any saved card at the time of checkout for any vendor, allowing for an easier, more seamless shopping experience.

It is of utmost importance for LAShowroom.com to protect private information, especially cardholder data as per guidelines set by the PCI Security Standards Council. We require all participating vendors to strictly adhere to PCI DSS guidelines whenever confidential credit card data is being handled.

These new features will continue to undergo constant surveillance and improvement by LA Showroom to ensure the highest level of security for the buyers.

If you have any further questions, please do not hesitate to reach out to our service team at 213-627-0339, or submit a query via contact form. Thank you for your attention and support.
